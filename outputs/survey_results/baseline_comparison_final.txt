BASELINE 2: MARKOV CHAIN (1st-order)
─────────────────────────────────────────
Algorithm: 
  • Learn transition probabilities P(note_t | note_{t-1})
  • Generate by sampling from learned transition matrix
  
Training Data: 800 synthetic music sequences
Parameters: 128 states (MIDI pitches), learnable transitions
Training Time: ~50ms
Characteristics:
  • Local coherence: respects pitch transitions from training
  • Limited lookahead: ignores long-term structure
  • Some repetition: learned patterns repeat
  • Basic musicality: some structure, but limited

Transition Matrix Statistics:
  • States with transitions: 102 / 128 (80%)
  • Average transitions per state: 8.2 (low connectivity)
  • Smoothness: ~60% of next-note transitions differ by ≤5 semitones
