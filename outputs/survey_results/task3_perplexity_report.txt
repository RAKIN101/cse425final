TASK 3 - TRANSFORMER ARCHITECTURE
Perplexity Evaluation Report

=========================================
MODEL ARCHITECTURE
=========================================
Architecture: Transformer-based Sequence-to-Sequence Model
Components:
  • Token Embedding: vocab_size=128, d_model=128
  • Positional Encoding: max_len=256 (sinusoidal)
  • Transformer Encoder: 2 layers, 4 attention heads
  • Feed-forward: dim_feedforward=256 per layer
  • Causal Attention Mask: Prevents attending to future tokens
  • Output Layer: Linear projection to vocab_size (128)

Training Configuration:
  • Optimizer: Adam (lr=0.001)
  • Loss Function: Cross-Entropy Loss
  • Batch Size: 32
  • Sequence Length: 64 tokens
  • Total Epochs: 10
  • Dataset: 800 synthetic music sequences
